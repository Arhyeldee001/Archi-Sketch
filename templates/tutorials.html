Archi Trace Tutorials

Learn how to use Archi Trace to trace effectively

Getting Started

After sign up or login, you would be prompted to your dashboard where you proceed to the tracer app as shown above.

Payment

To use Archi Trace you need to subscribe for a token of ₦500 for 3 days. Select your plan and pay as shown above.

Allow Camera

Archi Trace uses your camera to help you trace effectively. Allow camera access for the best experience.

Import Image

After successful payment and camera access, upload your image and start tracing as shown above.

Successful Import

When you import your image it will appear like this on the screen. Then move to the next step.

Preparation

Place your phone on a stand or tumbler above your paper for stability as illustrated above.

Alignment & Scaling

Reduce opacity to see through your image, drag/pinch/zoom to scale, and use the grid lines for alignment.

Import More Projects

You can also use the left panel to import additional images.

Enjoy the Experience

Ensure your stand is stable. Beginners should trace slowly to get used to the rhythm of drawing. Enjoy!
