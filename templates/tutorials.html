Archi Trace Tutorials

Learn how to use Archi Trace to trace effectively

Getting Started

After sign up or login, you would be propted to your dashboard where you would proceed to the tracer app as shown above.

Payment

To use Archi Trace you would have to subscribe for a little token of 500 NGN for 3 days, select you plan and pay as shown above.

Allow Camera

Archi Trace uses your camera to help you trace effectively for the best experience, so allow camera access and proceed.

Import image

Upon successful payment and camera access, you would gain full access to the app where you would upload your image and trace as shown above.

Successful Import

When you import your image it will appear as such on the screen and then you move to next step.

Prepration

Get a phone stand, tumbler or any object with a prefareble vertical height and place it on your paper, place the phone on top of the cup as illustrated above.

Alignment and Scaling

On your phone reduce the opacity to let you see through the image you want to Trace, drag, pinch, zoom to perfared scale and alignment, u can activte the grid line in the right panel to help with that.

Import other projects

You can also use the left panel to import more images.

Enjoy the experience

It is important to make sure your cup or phone stand is stable, and for beginners it is advice to trace slowly to fully get aquinted with the rhythm of drawing, Enjoy!!.
